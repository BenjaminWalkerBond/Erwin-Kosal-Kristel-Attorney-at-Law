My Philosophy

Client Centered Representation

I provide client-centered legal representation. Client-centered representation means always focusing on what my clients tell me their interests are and not imposing on them what I believe their interests should be. When we first meet, I will not only discuss the legal issues with you, I will also strive to understand your priorities and any impact legal proceedings, whether it be criminal or civil, will have on your life. We will then together decide the best way forward. It is your life, so you need to be in control.I will advocate aggressively on your behalf. I know the importance of providing guidance and advice but understand that the case belongs to you. I am dedicated to transparency and keeping clients informed throughout the course of representation.‍

My Philosophy

Flexible Representation

When you retain the Law Office of Erwin Kosal Kristel, you enjoy the benefits of working with a solo practitioner. Unlike some of the bigger law firms, your case will not be moved around between lawyers.
